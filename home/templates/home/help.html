Help & Information

Registration & Login Requirements

To use the Ambulance Detection System, you must first register an account or log in if you already have one.

Click the "Register" button and fill in the required details. If you already have an account, simply click "Login".

About the Ambulance Detection System

The Ambulance Detection System helps in detecting ambulances in real-time and prioritizing emergency vehicles for safety and efficiency.

About the Ambulance Service System

The Ambulance Service System provides emergency response coordination, ensuring quick dispatch of ambulances to critical locations.
